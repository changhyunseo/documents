title: If blocks
---

HTML doesn't have a way of expressing *logic*, like conditionals and loops. Svelte does.

To conditionally render some markup, we wrap it in an `if` block:

```html
{#if user.loggedIn}
	<button on:click={toggle}>
		Log out
	</button>
{/if}

{#if !user.loggedIn}
	<button on:click={toggle}>
		Log in
	</button>
{/if}
```
